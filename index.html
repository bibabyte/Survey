Feelings Tracker

The purpose of this tracker is to gather data over time about how you are feeling, emotionally, physically, and track a level of mindfulness. The tracker was inspired in part by the circumflex model of affect (Russell, 1980). The idea that one could track their affect over time with a simple scale and two factors (happiness and activity) is a simple and straightforward way of seeing feelings as they manifest inside oneself over time.
I have added mindfulness and pain as two additional criteria to give a bigger picture. If you have any other criteria you would like to see tracked please send me a message via email.
